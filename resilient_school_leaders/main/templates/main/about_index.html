About Resilient School Leaders

Resilient School Leaders (RSL) is an open-source web application designed to provide school leaders with a modern, easy-to-navigate resource hub. Our platform serves as a centralized platform for accessing a wide range of resources across various categories.

With RSL, school leaders can find support and guidance in areas such as professional and leadership development, well-being and resilience, diversity equity and inclusion, team building, and community networking.

We understand the challenges that school leaders face in their roles, and our goal is to empower them by providing the necessary tools and information to excel in their professional and academic endeavors.

What We Offer

Personal Development
