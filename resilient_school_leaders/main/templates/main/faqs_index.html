Resilient School Leaders (RSL) is an open-source site designed to serve as a resource hub for school leaders and professionals. It provides easy access to a wide range of resources from various categories, including professional and leadership development, well-being and resilience, diversity equity and inclusion, team building, and community and networking. The platform offers a user-friendly interface to navigate through these resources effectively.

Resilient School Leaders tried to implement an easy to navigate and user-friendly interface so you can find your resources as easily as possible. Everything is acessible in one click. Use the navigation bar to find available resources, view all of them or filter by category. Up to you!

Yes, you can contribute resources to Resilient School Leaders. If you have valuable content or know of useful resources, you can submit them through the platform's contribution process. The website's administrators will review and curate the submissions to ensure they align with the platform's goals. Please reach out to us with the recommended resource through the Contact Page.

To stay informed about the latest resources, updates, and news from Resilient School Leaders, you can subscribe to the platform's newsletter. Additionally, follow our official social media channels for regular updates and announcements.

Your feedback is valuable to us! To provide feedback or report any issues with the website, you can use the "Contact Us" page or the provided feedback form. Our team will review your message and respond as soon as possible.

Resilient School Leaders was conceptualized by a team of educational professionals who recognized the need for a centralized resource hub for school leaders. The idea originated from a desire to empower school leaders with valuable resources and support to enhance their leadership skills and promote resilience within the education community.
